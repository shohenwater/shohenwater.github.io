About Me

I am an Actuarial Science graduate from Northern Illinois University. In my regression and time series analysis classes I learned that I had talent and enjoyed working with data. Post-graduation I worked to gain Python and SQL skills to acquire a broad skill set to collect, clean, investigate, and create easily understandable visuals.

While doing this, I worked as a substitute teacher where I was able to improve upon my ability to communicate ideas to novice audiences and earned a long-term substitute position. In this position I was able to work both individually and as part of a team to problem solve through the COVID-19 situation and help my special needs students.

I have always been a curious person and have appreciated the unexpected insights that working with data allows. My personal goal is to always expand my abilities and learn new ideas. Ideally, I would love to be employed somewhere where the work I do will improve the lives of many people and I can work on big problems that need big ideas to solve.

I love to talk data whenever I can. Feel free to email me at shohenwater@gmail.com to talk about any of my analyses or data in general!
